About Naamcol

This was the First Shipchandling Company in Kenya and was Established in 1888

Our Story

Founded in 1888 by Ali Mohamed El Kilifi in Mombasa, Kenya, Muses Mohamed began as a small shipchandling business catering to the maritime needs of the coastal town. Over the years, the company expanded its services, becoming a trusted name in the maritime industry.

Through the dedication of the second and third generations, Muses Mohamed expanded its reach and diversified its services. The company became known for its commitment to quality and reliability, serving Navy, Cruise, and Cargo ships with a comprehensive range of supplies and services.

Today, under the leadership of the fourth generation, Naamcol continues to build on its rich heritage, offering exceptional marine and distribution services. With a strong focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we are recognized as a leader in the industry, delivering reliable solutions across the globe.
