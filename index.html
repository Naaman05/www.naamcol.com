Welcome to Naamcol

Naamcol is an integral part of Muses Mohamed, a company with a rich history dating back to 1888. As a fourth-generation company, Naamcol continues the legacy of excellence established by its founders, offering specialized marine and distribution services. Our commitment to quality and innovation drives our success across the globe, providing reliable solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Expertise

Shipchandling Services

Muses Mohamed has been a pioneer in providing exceptional shipchandling services in Mombasa. We ensure every vessel is fully equipped for smooth operations, catering to Navy, Cruise, and Cargo ships.

Import and Export Services

Our Import and Export division specializes in facilitating smooth and efficient trade across international borders, ensuring safe and timely delivery of goods.

Wholesale Distribution

Naamcol is a leading distributor in the region, known for distributing Tropical Heat products throughout the Coast of Kenya with a robust and reliable network.
