Our Products

Naaman Muses and Co. Ltd is a leading distributor of various high-quality consumer goods and products from well-known brands. We specialize in distributing products from reputable manufacturers, ensuring that our customers have access to the best products in the market.

Tropical Heat Products

Known for its extensive range of snacks, spices, and condiments, Tropical Heat is a favorite among consumers. We proudly distribute a variety of snacks, including potato crisps, peanuts, chevda, and a range of spices and seasonings.

Pwani Oil Products Ltd

As a distributor of Pwani Oil Products, we supply a range of cooking oils, fats, and soaps. Their popular products, like Fresh Fri cooking oil and Salit cooking oil, are favorites in both household kitchens and commercial establishments.

Manji Food Industries

Manji is a leading manufacturer of biscuits and baked goods. We distribute a comprehensive range of their products, including cream biscuits, glucose biscuits, and assorted cookies, ensuring access to these tasty treats across East Africa.

Kimfay

Specializing in personal hygiene and sanitary products, Kimfay is a trusted name. We distribute a range of their products, including sanitary pads, tissues, and baby diapers, meeting the needs of consumers looking for reliable hygiene products.

Trufoods

Trufoods is known for its extensive range of food products, including jams, sauces, and beverages. We ensure these popular items, such as fruit jams, tomato sauce, and peanut butter, are readily available through our extensive distribution network.
