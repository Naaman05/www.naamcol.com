Shipchandling Services

Muses Mohamed has been a pioneer in providing exceptional shipchandling services in Mombasa. With decades of experience in the maritime industry, we ensure that every vessel is fully equipped with the necessary supplies for smooth operations. From provisions to technical equipment, we cater to Navy, Cruise, and Cargo ships, ensuring a reliable and efficient service that meets the highest standards of safety and quality.

Import and Export Services

Our Import and Export division specializes in facilitating smooth and efficient trade across international borders. With a vast network of global partners and a deep understanding of customs regulations, Naamcol ensures the safe and timely delivery of goods. We handle all types of cargo, providing a seamless experience for our clients, whether they are importing goods into Kenya or exporting to global markets.

Wholesale Distribution

Naamcol is a leading distributor in the region, particularly known for being the main distributor of Tropical Heat products throughout the Coast of Kenya. Our wholesale distribution network is extensive, ensuring that quality products reach every corner of the region. We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver promptly and efficiently, maintaining strong relationships with both suppliers and customers.
